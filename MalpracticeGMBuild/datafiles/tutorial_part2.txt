When you are in the medicine room, hover over a medication to receive basic information about the drug.
If you still want more information about a drug, click on the computer to get more information.
Using the computer takes time. For you, time is money. 
Make sure to use the computer when you want to know exactly what symptoms a medication treats and causes.
Some medications are used to treat symptoms that are pretty different from their general uses.
If you are confused, try looking it up.
